Grand Hotel

Snøhetta
South Tyrol, Italy
2026
Brief

Snøhetta's Grand Hotel is a contemporary reading of a familiar Alpine typology, designed with G22 projects for an invited competition in South Tyrol.

The monumental symmetry of the classic grand hotel is broken into separate volumes, stepped into the natural slope so each keeps its own view and its own light — the grandeur held, but loosened.

We illustrated it across the seasons — the same arcaded volumes in autumn, under snow, through a summer afternoon — framing the building against the mountains without letting either recede.

The bel étage

A grand staircase climbs to the bel étage, the heart of the hotel — the bar, the fireplace lounge over the forest, the restaurant opening onto the gardens.

Above it, 95 rooms reach out to balconies set between the timber façade and the inner core.
